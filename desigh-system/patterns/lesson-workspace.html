Learning flow

  1. Show prerequisites, estimated time, objectives, and completion rules before substantive content.
  2. Auto-save meaningful position and interaction state without claiming completion.
  3. Open a source reference in the adjacent pane, emphasize its authorized line range, and keep the lesson scroll position.
  4. Glossary definitions appear inline or as non-modal popovers and return focus to the term.
  5. Mark completion explicitly; assessments provide separate evidence of mastery.

Multi-step code tours

01 · ControllerAccept intent
02 · EventDurable handoff
03 · ConsumerCoordinate work

Each step includes purpose, file/symbol/lines, revision, and previous/next controls. The tree, lesson objective, and tour position stay visible or recoverable.
