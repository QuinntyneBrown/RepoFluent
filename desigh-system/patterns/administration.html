Retention and destructive workflows

Deletion requires exact scope

Name source content, derived indexes, caches, analytics, audit/legal exceptions, effective time, and recovery window before confirmation.

  • Use preview counts only when authorization and minimum-group rules allow them.
  • Require recent authentication or elevated approval for sensitive bulk actions.
  • After execution, provide an audit identifier and whether recovery remains possible.
