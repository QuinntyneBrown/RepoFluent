Curriculum lifecycle

Authors move a standardized, agent-produced package through safe upload, deterministic validation, preview, human review, publication, versioning, and retirement.

Publication and retirement

  • Publication confirmation names tenant, curriculum, source revision, audience visibility, and the immutable version to create.
  • Learners cannot see drafts or approved-but-unpublished versions. Assignments bind to a specific published version or an explicit policy.
  • Retirement stops future use while preserving prior assignments, progress, attempts, reports, and audit events.
  • Every upload, validation, approval, publication, rejection, export, and retirement records actor and timestamp.
