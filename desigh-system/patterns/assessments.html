Attempts, protection, and invalidation

  • History records curriculum version, item version, start/submission timestamps, score, outcome, and feedback-release state.
  • Before release, do not send answer keys, protected rationale, hidden pool contents, or correctness flags to the client.
  • When a reviewer invalidates a defective item, label the affected attempt, recalculate outcomes consistently, and retain the audit trail.
  • Incorrect formative answers link to exact review material without revealing unrelated protected answers.
