  • Permissions apply to every summary, chart, drill-down, search result, and export—not only the page route.
  • Avoid small-group inference through subtraction, adjacent filters, chart tooltips, or export totals.
  • Do not use analytics as punitive employee ranking; include context, acceptable-use guidance, and configurable indicators.

Comparison and export

  • Comparisons label cohort membership, curriculum version, time window, assignment policy, and population changes.
  • Exports include filter metadata, generated time, data version, column definitions, and authorization scope.
  • Stale, partial, recalculated, and invalidated data is explicitly marked and excluded consistently where required.
  • Content-quality measures such as abandonment and repeated failure lead to content review, not learner blame.
