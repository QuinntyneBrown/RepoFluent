Interaction and fallback

  • Arrow keys move spatially among nodes; Tab reaches toolbar, canvas, inspector, table, and related actions.
  • Zoom never changes semantic selection. “Fit graph” restores the full topology. Pan and zoom controls are labeled.
  • WebGPU may add ambient depth or large-graph performance, but initial content, selection, inspector, and table render without it.
  • On phone, nodes become an ordered list grouped by layer and the inspector follows selection; the relationship table remains available.
  • Large graphs progressively load or cluster while preserving search and the full accessible relationship model.
