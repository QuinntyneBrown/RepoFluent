Foundation / F04

Elevation & shape

Code Command is planar by default. Borders define working regions; shadows appear only when one surface physically interrupts another.

RF-F04

Elevation ladder

LEVEL 0

Canvas, sidebar, panel. No shadow.

LEVEL 1

Interactive card and tooltip.

LEVEL 2

Menu, popover, toast.

LEVEL 3

Modal dialog and command palette.

A darker surface and stronger border accompany elevation; a shadow alone must not be the only boundary.

Focus and selection

DO NOT CONFUSE THESE STATES
  • Focus is cyan and follows the keyboard cursor.
  • Selection uses a green edge or dim green fill and persists after focus moves.
  • Primary action uses a solid green fill and indicates hierarchy, not selection.

Usage rules

DO

Use lines and surface steps for durable layout; reserve shadows for temporary overlap.

DON'T

Round every container, stack multiple shadows, or allow selection styling to obscure focus.
