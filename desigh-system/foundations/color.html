Foundation / F01

Color

A low-luminance workspace with precise semantic accents. Color clarifies interaction, health, risk, and data relationships; it never carries meaning alone.

RF-F01

Surfaces

Five dark layers establish hierarchy primarily through boundaries. Avoid stacking shadows where a one-pixel line communicates the relationship.

DO

Pair every semantic color with an icon, label, state word, or structural change.

DON'T

Use green to decorate neutral content or red merely to make an element prominent.

Chart palette

Series receive fixed slots in order. Do not recycle a slot within one visualization, and keep warning/danger colors out of categorical charts unless they encode those states.

CATEGORICAL SLOTS / 01–08

Contrast contract

  • Normal text targets at least 4.5:1 against every supported surface; large text targets 3:1.
  • Focus indicators and essential control boundaries target at least 3:1 against adjacent colors.
  • State fills use their matching dark background and retain a written state label.
  • Charts provide labels, patterns or shapes where series distinction is essential, plus an equivalent table.
