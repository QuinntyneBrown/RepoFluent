Step 2 guidance

active tour step

The service intentionally reads like a pipeline. It first derives a subtotal from cart lines, then delegates discount logic to the injected registry, then calculates tax against the discounted amount and shipping address before returning immutable totals.

The important boundary is the IPromotionRuleRegistry. A pricing rule is not embedded in the service; rules are composed elsewhere and applied here so checkout remains extensible.

Glossary: pricing rule
A pricing rule is a small, registered policy that can modify a cart total: for example seasonal discounts, minimum-order promotions, or customer-tier adjustments. In Orion, rules are registered by PromotionRuleRegistry and applied as a set.
