## Mission and boundary

The subsystem provides the shared design tokens, interaction primitives, rendering capability strategy, responsive shell, accessibility baseline, and frontend performance controls used across learner, assessment, authoring, administration, and analytics flows. Domain screens own their content semantics; this subsystem owns consistent usable delivery.

## Quality requirements

- No required control, explanation, status, answer, or navigation affordance shall depend only on hover, animation, color, canvas, or GPU output.
- Components shall remain legible and operable under tenant branding and supported zoom/text-resize settings.
- Shared UI components shall publish accessibility and performance usage guidance and test coverage.
- Graceful degradation shall favor available semantic content over visual fidelity.

## Dependencies and exclusions

- Depends on domain-owned semantic requirements, browser-support policy, design governance, telemetry, and accessibility/performance test infrastructure.
- This subsystem does not define business authorization, grading, or analytics calculations.
