## Mission and boundary

The subsystem makes RepoFluent operable and supportable without exposing customer content unnecessarily. It owns telemetry conventions, cross-component correlation, service-level indicators, monitoring/alerts, safe diagnostics, retry/idempotency reliability controls, stale-job detection, backup/restore readiness, incident procedures, and production performance measurement. Domain subsystems remain responsible for emitting correct events at their boundaries.

## Quality requirements

- Telemetry schemas shall be versioned and use bounded-cardinality dimensions.
- Alerting shall favor actionable user-impact signals and include runbook/owner/escalation context.
- Observability failure shall not expose data or silently permit unsafe domain behavior.
- Reliability tests shall cover retries, duplication, out-of-order events, partial dependency failure, and recovery.
- Operational access and diagnostic export shall be authorized and audited.

## Dependencies and exclusions

- Depends on domain instrumentation, approved telemetry infrastructure, security/privacy controls, platform hosting, and on-call/support ownership.
- Exact SLO values beyond PRD targets, backup RPO/RTO, and deployment topology require production architecture decisions before baseline approval.
