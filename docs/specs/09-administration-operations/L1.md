## Mission and boundary

The subsystem provides authorized tenant operators with coherent controls for users, groups, roles, curricula, assignments, tenant settings, processing status, retention requests, branding, and notifications. It coordinates owner subsystems rather than bypassing their policies. Platform-internal observability and incident response are owned by Observability and Supportability.

## Quality requirements

- Administrative interfaces shall meet WCAG 2.2 AA for launch-critical flows and require confirmation for consequential operations.
- Bulk operations shall provide preview, per-item outcome, idempotency, and recoverable retry behavior.
- Tenant-facing diagnostics shall expose safe correlation IDs and status, not source excerpts, secrets, answer keys, tokens, or internal stack traces.
- Configuration changes shall use explicit versions or concurrency controls to prevent silent overwrite.

## Dependencies and exclusions

- Depends on all domain subsystems, central authorization/audit, notification delivery, retention orchestration, and operational status APIs.
- Customer identity-provider configuration outside RepoFluent, legal-hold decision making, and support staff access policy are external governance responsibilities.
