## Mission and boundary

The subsystem establishes cross-cutting controls that keep tenant code, documents, curriculum, assessments, learner activity, and operational evidence protected throughout collection, processing, delivery, retention, and deletion. It owns security/privacy policy, control requirements, threat and privacy reviews, security scanning standards, data classification, encryption expectations, audit integrity, and security-readiness gates. Domain subsystems implement their portion of these controls.

## Quality requirements

- Security controls shall default to deny or safe inert behavior when identity, policy, classification, validation, or key state is unknown.
- Security failures shall be observable without exposing protected payloads to clients, logs, or support users.
- Controls shall be verifiable through automated conformance tests and manual security/privacy review.
- Exceptions shall identify owner, scope, risk, compensating control, approval, and expiry.

## Dependencies and exclusions

- Depends on every subsystem, approved infrastructure/key/identity providers, legal/privacy guidance, and security operations.
- This specification does not assert a deployment model, certification, jurisdiction, or retention duration not resolved in PRD section 18.
